Terraria Modding

One of my favorite hobbies is to made mods for the best video game of all time, Terraria. Here are a few of the one that I have made.

TWAILA

A Terraria mod that displays information about the tile you are hovering over in the world.

Mod Page
Portal Cursor

Turns your mouse into the Portal cursor when using the portal gun. Adds indicators around the mouse that correlate to your portals in the world.

Mod Page
More Block Swap

Allows for more tiles to be block swapped with each other. Works for vanilla and modded tiles.

Mod Page
Calamity Rarities

Small Calamity addon mod that changes the pickup text color of certain Calamity items to match their tooltip colors.

Red Team Mod
